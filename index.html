Campus Life

laboratories

The School houses Labs equipped with apparatus and device to help impart the best training to the students. Care is taken that all the necessary measures are taken for their safety. Physics, chemistry, Biology and Maths Labs facilitates practical understanding of the concepts. The labs are designed so as to comply with the norms prescribed by CBSE.

school library

The library caters to the informational needs of its students. A huge collection of classified text books, reference books, newspapers, comic tales from epics, maps, pictorials, cassettes, life histories and autobiographies of national heroes and freedom fighter are available for their wade. It is also equipped with a wide range of educational and informative CD's and Encyclopedias. A constant endeavor is made to enrich the Library with latest books.

school computer lab

The school owns an air conditioned and specious computer lab. It offers high end courses in computer Science to become proficient in the field, under the guidance of Expert Faculty.
